About the Research Unit

This platform highlights the research, innovation and data science work being developed within the hospital to support clinical excellence, evidence generation and better patient outcomes.

Our research unit works at the intersection of clinical care, data science, biostatistics and digital health. The goal is to create strong systems for registries, research datasets, analytics and AI deployment in the hospital ecosystem.

We support investigators and departments in planning, executing and scaling high-impact research projects by bringing together clinicians, researchers, data scientists and technology teams.

  • Clinical registry development and governance
  • Research data management and analytics support
  • AI and predictive modeling initiatives
  • Cross-functional collaboration with clinical and IT teams

Roadmap & Milestones

A simple timeline section that can be updated as work progresses.

Phase 1 - Identify Priority Registry Areas

Finalize ICU, Hematology and AMR as the first focused registry areas and align stakeholders.

Phase 2 - Clinical and IT Alignment

Meet with clinical teams and IT stakeholders to define EMR dependencies, workflows and data requirements.

Phase 3 - Data Governance Policy

Draft and review a research data governance policy to support secure and standardized data use.

Phase 4 - AI Use Case Identification

Shortlist hospital-ready AI opportunities and build pilots in selected clinical pathways.
